Tobitatenai Watashi ni Anata ga Tsubasa wo Kureta (2004)
Tobitatenai Watashi ni Anata ga Tsubasa wo Kureta (飛び立てない私にあなたが翼をくれた, lit. "You Gave Me Wings When I Couldn't Fly") is the second ending theme of Ultraman Nexus.

Artist: U-ka Saegusa IN db
Released: 2004
Album: Ultraman Nexus Original Soundtrack Vol 1
